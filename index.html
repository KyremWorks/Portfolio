Who I am

I'm a solo developer from Italy. Gone from passion to a life style.

It began with curiosity: I observed how things worked, and tried making my own. Curiosity turned into modding, modding turned into real games. Small, humor dense pixel worlds I could call mine.

Now I create experiences and tell it out loud with each devlog, game jam and rough prototype. A life long ongoing project.
