1. Who is the data controller

This site (kyremworks.github.io) is a personal portfolio run by KyremWorks, an independent developer based in Italy. The site is not operated by a registered company: the data controller under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) is the individual operating under the name "KyremWorks".

Contact: kyremworks.contact@gmail.com, or via the contact form on this site.

2. What data is processed, and how

  • Data you provide voluntarily through the contact form: name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Technical data collected automatically: your IP address and browser/device data, collected by hCaptcha for anti-bot verification (active on every visit to the site, not only when the form is submitted); your IP address and standard request logs collected by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and by the CDN that serves the site's technical libraries (unpkg.com) and one portfolio project image (glitchvalley.it).
  • Local preference: your light/dark theme choice is saved in your browser's localStorage. It stays on your device, is never sent to us, and is not a cookie.
  • This site does not use any analytics, statistics, or profiling tools (no Google Analytics or similar), and does not send marketing emails.

Retention: messages sent through the contact form are forwarded to a dedicated inbox and kept there only for as long as needed to read and respond to them; you can request deletion at any time (see "Your rights" below). Web3Forms, the service that delivers the form, does not retain submission content long-term either; see "Third parties" below.

3. Legal basis for processing

  • Contact form: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), freely given by filling in and submitting the form.
  • hCaptcha (anti-spam): our legitimate interest in protecting the site from abuse and spam (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Hosting/CDN technical logs: the legitimate interest of the site and of our providers in keeping the site secure, available, and working correctly (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Theme preference: a technical detail strictly necessary for the interface to work; it stays on your device and doesn't require a specific legal basis.

4. Purposes of processing

  • Responding to messages, questions, or collaboration requests sent through the contact form.
  • Preventing spam and abuse of the contact form.
  • Keeping the site technically available, secure, and working correctly.
  • Remembering your light/dark theme choice between visits.

No direct marketing, automated profiling, or sale/sharing of data with third parties for their own purposes.

Some of the providers listed above are based in, or run infrastructure in, the United States (Web3Forms, hCaptcha/Intuition Machines, GitHub Pages, unpkg.com). These transfers outside the EU/EEA rely on the safeguards those providers themselves put in place, typically Standard Contractual Clauses and/or participation in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework where applicable. Please refer to each provider's own privacy policy for up-to-date details: Web3Forms, hCaptcha, GitHub.

7. Your rights

Under Articles 15-22 GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request its correction or deletion ("right to be forgotten");
  • Request that processing be restricted;
  • Request data portability;
  • Object to processing;
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before the withdrawal.

You can also lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali, www.garanteprivacy.it).

How we actually fulfil these requests: contact form submissions pass through Web3Forms and land in our own inbox; we don't use a database or separate storage system. If you ask us to access, correct, or delete your data, we search that inbox for messages from you and act on them directly; Web3Forms itself does not keep a long-term copy of submissions on its end (see "Third parties" above). We will respond within the timeframe required by law (normally within one month).

To exercise these rights: kyremworks.contact@gmail.com

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy at any time, for example if the site starts using new tools or services. Changes will be published on this same page, with the "last updated" date above revised accordingly. Since this site has no newsletter or user accounts, there is no separate notification system. Please check back here periodically.
